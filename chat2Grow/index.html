What are the requirements?
Chat2Grow helps you to keep things simple! All that you need is:
A web browser - to help access the Chat2Grow platform.
A mobile phone number - to become your WhatsApp number
A WhatsApp installed mobile device - to activate your WhatsApp number
 
Can the same number be used for personal and Chat2Grow ?
It is recommended to assign a number exclusively for Chat2Grow to interact with your customers.
 
Is it possible to recover the WhatsApp account ?
Definitely Yes! Should you choose to switch to a different WhataApp number or in the improbable possibility of you wanting to discontinue our service, you can recover the account in a simple manner.
 
Who is an agent?
Agents are your customer support executives, who will be making use of the Chat2Grow platform to facilitate better customer care experience. The agents can handle customers in a parallel fashion and are backed the process simplification tools provided by Chat2Grow.
 
How is Chat2Grow priced?
The service is priced with relation to the number of agents making use of the platform. The cost per agent assigned to your Chat2Grow account can be seen on our pricing page. Please note: Should you need any customization or the addition of a specific feature, kindly clarify the same with us by just dropping a mail. We will get in touch !
How do I get started?
The signup is a very simple process. The login option on the top right corner of the page should come handy. It will lead you to the sign-up page. The process will assist you with the sequential procedure of getting started. Our customer support team is always available to assist you. Should you have any query, please drop us a mail.
 
What is the process to add more agents?
The objective of Chat2Grow is to simply and help you stay in control of the customer support process. Agents can be added,managed and supervised through the admin panel. We are happy to provide further clarifications, please get in touch !
